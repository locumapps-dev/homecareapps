Post-Surgical Wound Care at Home

Professional wound dressing and infection monitoring by a verified registered nurse — across Klang Valley.

45–60 min From RM180 Registered Ward Nurse

Our registered nurse visits your home to assess the wound, cleanse the site, and apply a fresh sterile dressing. Every visit includes infection monitoring and a post-visit clinical report emailed to you — shareable with your doctor or specialist.

What the nurse does

  • Full wound assessment
  • Cleansing and sterile dressing change
  • Infection monitoring
  • Post-visit clinical report

Suitable for

  • Post-surgery patients (abdominal, C-section, orthopaedic)
  • Diabetic foot ulcers
  • Pressure sores
  • Post-discharge wound care

Prescribed dressings from hospital pharmacy Clean flat surface with good lighting Hospital discharge summary (if available)
