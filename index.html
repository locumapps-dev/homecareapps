Questions families ask

Everything you need to know before booking your first home nursing visit.

A registered nurse holds a degree in nursing and is licensed by the Malaysian Nursing Board to perform clinical procedures — wound dressing, IV therapy, medication injection. A caregiver provides personal assistance like bathing and mobility. HomeCareApps only provides registered nurses.
Yes. Every nurse on HomeCareApps holds a valid Annual Practising Certificate (APC) from the Malaysian Nursing Board. We verify credentials before any nurse is approved and track APC expiry dates automatically.
